Our Vsion and Mission

Vision

We encourage more people around the world to contribute and experience the impact of their contributions towards this pandemic.

Mission

To gather funds in support of all countries to respond to Covid-19, especially in countries with less access to global markets and lower resources.

About Us

In collaboration with the Covid-19 Solidarity Response Fund, the funds collected are to support WHO's Covid-19 effort in fighting this invisible war.

The pandemic is impacting communities everywhere. It’s never been more urgent to support the global response, led by the World Health Organization (WHO).

Some donations also support additional Fund partners like UNICEF, the World Food Programme, the UN’s refugee agency UNHCR, and the vaccine development alliance CEPI.
